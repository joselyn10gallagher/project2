Benefits of Special Effects

  • Practical Special Effects creates a sense of realism on set. The actors can feel like they live in the movie's imaginary world, and can enhance their acting. Also, worlds such as Willy Wonka & The Chocolate Factory, Jurassic Park, and Star Wars makes the audience wish that they were apart of those movies.


  • SFX encourages creativity. There's a lot of techniques involved in creating scenes which makes the idea of movie-making special.
